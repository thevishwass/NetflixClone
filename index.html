Frequently Asked Questions

What is Netflix?
Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more on thousands of internet-connected devices.
How much does Netflix cost?
Netflix offers different pricing plans starting at $9.99 per month, depending on your country and selected plan.
Where can I watch?
You can watch Netflix on any internet-connected device that offers the Netflix app, such as smart TVs, gaming consoles, smartphones, tablets, and streaming media players.
How do I cancel?
You can cancel your Netflix subscription anytime by going to your account settings and selecting the cancel option. There are no cancellation fees.
What can I watch on Netflix?
Netflix offers an extensive library of feature films, documentaries, TV shows, anime, and more, including Netflix originals like Stranger Things, The Witcher, and Squid Game.
